ADMISSION

For the registrations of the new academic session August 2016

Steps for the admission:

  1. Registration forms
  2. Aptitude test for Pre-Junior level
  3. Admission test for Grade 1 and up
  4. Interview with the Principal
  5. Admission Order on the basis of the test and interview

Requirements:
  1. Birth Certificate
    • Please bring along the original birth certificate (this will be returned)
    • You are required to submit a photocopy of the same.
  2. Two passport sized photographs
  3. Progress report of the previous school (if any)
  4. Leaving Certificate of the previous school
