Afzal Grammar School

Welcome to Afzal Grammer School. We are a young and ambitious school with a simple aim: to provide your child with an outstanding educational experience. We are members of COIS, the Confederation of International Schools, and are in the process of planning our first school inspection by ISI, the Independent Schools Inspectorate, from the Islamabad. We are also registered as a school, and fully regulated, by the government. We follow the National Curriculum for Pakistan, enhanced to cover all aspects of the national curriculum requirements.
