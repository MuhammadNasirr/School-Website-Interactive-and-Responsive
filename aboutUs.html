ABOUT US

Afzal Grammar School was established in 2005 with the aim and objective of transferring the knowledge, understanding and social, cultural and moral values to the new generation. We probe to expand the horizon of knowledge provided to the children constantly and consistently. We are proud that we nurtured our children into disciplined and well groomed individuals. We are confident that we shall continue to deliver the same with such an unmatchable, impeccable and progressive attitude of the team of AGS.

Individual attention is given to the children as we have deliberately maintained a low child–teacher ratio. Children are more secure and confident in terms of their academics as the teachers are always approachable and helpful. We instill in them the love for learning and exploring. We believe in comprehensive studies which shall help them through out their lives. Knowledge is divulged in the most contemporary manner to make it effective and implicit.

AGS has the finest team of teachers. Their finesse and talent is prominently shown in the work and the attitude of the children.

To ensure the improvement in our children, regular evaluations are made and we maintain a graph to assess the progress of each child. We not only focus on the academic progress of the children but we improve their overall outlook towards the studies by making it interesting, competitive and exciting. To make their efforts worthwhile we have regular quiz, competitions and programs which the children love and look forward to.

We have a well balanced curriculum, designed carefully by keeping in mind the mental growth and development of each age group. Our curriculum, co-curricular and extra curricular activities are designed to enable children compete in every walk of life in future. We want our children to be all rounder. For this a strong bond is maintained between, parent, administration and child.
